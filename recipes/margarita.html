Margarita

Description

A margarita, the quintessential cocktail for those craving a taste of Mexico, is a vibrant symphony of flavors that dances on the palate. Crafted with premium tequila, the heart and soul of this drink, it delivers a smooth yet spirited kick that awakens the senses. Freshly squeezed lime juice provides a zesty tang, perfectly complementing the tequila's warmth, while a touch of orange liqueur adds a subtle sweetness and depth. Served in a salt-rimmed glass, each sip is a tantalizing journey of citrusy brightness, tingling spice, and a hint of tropical escape, making it the ultimate indulgence for any fiesta or leisurely evening.

Estimated Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

Yields 1 drink

  • 2 ounces blanco tequila
  • Juice of 1 whole lime
  • Â½ ounce triple sec, preferably Cointreau
  • Salt for rim (optional)
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add tequila, lime juice and triple sec.
  2. Shake until combined.
  3. Pour, with the ice, into a highball or footed glass (salt rim first if you like). Garnish with wedge of lime.
